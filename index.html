My Favorite Things

Simple things that make my heart smile.


My Family

I love spending time with my family. My kids are fun ages: almost six and almost two. My husband and I are so blessed.


Traveling

Who doesn't love taking trips? Vacations are a necessity for me. After so long of my day-to-day routines, my mind and body need to refresh. The best thing about traveling is trying new food. I also love to get pictures and videos of beautiful views.



Beaches

Beaches are my happy place. I'm pretty sure I was not supposed to be born in the Midwest. I love beaches, mountains, and warm weather. 80% of my travels are to beach destinations.



Cookie Dough

Cookie dough is my guilty pleasure. I love cookie dough icecream (the only ice cream I eat), milkshakes, or raw cookie dough! If I'm baking cookies, you can bet that half of the dough will not make it to the oven!



Wine

I am a self-proclaimed cheap wine connoisseur. Wine is always my drink of choice but I have my limits. I'm not willing to spend more than about $20 on a bottle of wine. One day I will treat myself to an expensive, vintage Chardonnay. I love going to wineries or wine bars for tastings when I travel.


Purple

For as long as I can remember, purple has always been my favorite color. I tend to buy lots of purple for my daughter and me.



Seafood

I'm a (sometimes) pescatarian. I go long periods of time without eating meat. But I cannot give up seafood.
